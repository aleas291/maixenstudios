    Perfect-Job

    your company is doing better day by day and you are soon going to be the leading industry in your field. But, you are actually a spy and are their rival and you are here to
    slow them down as much as possible. Having been ordered by your boss, you successfully infiltrated Ordinary corp and now you need to use every way you can to slow them down, be it by hacking their comupter, burning stuff or even resorting to more radical actions...
    will you be able to get your next paycheck by staying undetected?
    only time will tell.

    this project was made for the Brackeys Game Jam 2025.1

    the Brackeys game jam runs twice a year and is a good way to meet other devs, to talk about their game and their progress. This time the challenge was to make a game ine one wee on the Theme Nothing can go Wrong
